Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering

The Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering Programme of Walchand Institute of Technology was established in the year 1999 with the intake of 40 seats. In 2002, the intake was increased to 60 seats while in 2010, the total intake of 120 seats was sanctioned. The program runs in two shifts since 2010.It has grown over the years into a competent wing with state of the art computing facilities, well equipped laboratories and dedicated faculty. The Programme is now recognized as a research.
